PROJECTS

Phone Call Track

Phone Call Track is designed to keep track all the phone calls that client makes and receives from other people. The program will keep track with other information such as name, phone number, date, and time. Phone Call Track is later upgraded to Phone Bill App which is included next.

Phone Bill App

Phone Bill App is the final phase from Phone Call Track. Phone Bill App is lauched by Android OS. In Phone Bill App, customer can have all the features from Phone Call Track, search from range of date, add, and remove phone bills from multiples customers.
